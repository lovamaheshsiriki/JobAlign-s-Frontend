About JobAlign

JobAlign is a community-driven project designed to revolutionize how job seekers and employers connect.

Our Mission

We believe that finding the right job or the right candidate shouldn't be a matter of luck. By leveraging AI-powered matching, comprehensive preparation resources, and intuitive user experiences, we're building a platform that creates meaningful connections between talent and opportunity.

Community-Driven

As a community project, JobAlign is built by developers, designers, and hiring professionals who understand the challenges of modern recruiting. We're committed to creating an open, transparent platform that serves both job seekers and employers equally.

Key Features

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with user-friendly design to provide smart resume parsing, intelligent job matching, comprehensive interview preparation, and powerful employer analytics - all designed to make hiring more efficient and effective.
